# AGENTS.md

This repository's operating guide for AI coding agents lives in [CLAUDE.md](./CLAUDE.md). Read it first and follow it in full.

It covers: the Staff LLM/AI Research Engineer role; the deep-research survey workflow (`surveys/<slug>/` layout, `order.json` + `references.md`, the `viewer/tools/` toolchain, the `/check-survey` sign-off gate); the capture-workflow conventions for `prompts/` (conversation logs), `todos/`, `decisions/`, `bugs/` (with the authoritative bug-severity guide), and `field-notes/`; the wired validation hooks under `.claude/hooks/`; the on-demand rule files `.claude/rules/math-authoring.md` and `.claude/rules/citation-integrity.md`; and the available skills and commands under `.claude/`.
