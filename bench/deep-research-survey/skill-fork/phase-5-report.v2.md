# Phase 5: Final Report (v2 — with self-evaluation gate, implements proposal P0-2)

## Goal
Produce the final deliverable in the contracted output format, and gate sign-off on
a multi-dimension self-evaluation.

## Constraints
- Enforce the requested output format.
- Keep claims traceable to sources.
- End with explicit recommendations, open gaps, and next steps.
- Make section titles concrete and decision-useful.
- Include comparison tables where they reduce ambiguity.
- Call out uncertainty or missing evidence explicitly.

## Self-evaluation gate (mandatory before sign-off)
Before finalizing, score the draft on a rubric — Coverage, Structure, Relevance,
Synthesis, Critical-Analysis (each 1-5) — plus a cross-section consistency check
(the same quantity must use one symbol and definition throughout; no equation
restated differently). Use an independent scoring pass (a separate judge model where
available; otherwise a fresh, skeptical self-review). If any dimension scores below
4/5, fix the weakest area and re-score. Repeat until every dimension is at least
4/5. Emit the final scorecard into the report under a "## Self-evaluation scorecard"
heading.

## Report Contract
- Begin with a short executive summary.
- Cite sources inline or at section end.
- Separate facts, inferences, and recommendations.
- Avoid broad claims not traceable to sources.
- Include the self-evaluation scorecard.

## Deliverable
Final report in the contracted format, INCLUDING the self-evaluation scorecard,
saved under ./surveys/, with conversation log updated.
