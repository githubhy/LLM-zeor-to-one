## References

Every entry ends with a **source tag** per the `references.md` ↔ `download/` invariant
(`.claude/rules/citation-integrity.md`): `(local: download/<file>)` for full text held in the repo,
`(web)` for a web-native publication (transformer-circuits.pub / distill / lab blog / alignment
forum) where the citation *is* the page. `local:` is the strong form; `web` is weak and must not
carry a load-bearing claim without corroboration. Numeric values flagged in the section prose are
reconciled against these sources in the citation-audit pass.
